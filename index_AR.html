CHI’s primary care clinic is seeking a skilled Naturopathic Doctor (ND) to complement and augment its collaborative and innovative team. CHI serves a diverse population in the Greater Washington, DC metro area and is committed to leveraging our collective knowledge and skills to improve mind, body and spirit. For more information: www.caseyhealth.org.

وصف الدور الوظيفي :

The role of the Naturopathic Doctor (ND) is a full-time position that incorporates the delivery of naturopathic medical services, patient care, oversight of the on-site dispensary and the integrative training program. The ND provides patient care in a team-based primary care and integrative health environment and is an integral part of the patient care team, working in a collaborative fashion with all providers. The ND consults with patients to offer a variety of naturopathic therapeutics such as dietary counseling, lifestyle counseling, natural medicines, and naturopathic physical medicine. The ND promotes health and wellness of CHI patients and helps other CHI providers include naturopathic recommendations into care, as appropriate. This role includes working together with the clinical team to maintain efficient and effective workflows, and supporting an environment of patient safety and satisfaction.
