Prince Sultan Military Medical City is one of the largest military medical cities in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It includes all medical specialties and subspecialties and has become a center of excellence and reference for all Armed Forces Medical Services Hospitals on many medical and research matters. It has also had a leading record in many medical achievements. Thanks to Allah, the unlimited support from the leaders of this country and the efforts exerted by PSMMC trained medical staff, several rare and complicated surgeries were performed in PSMMC. These ranged from adult liver transplantation, pediatric liver transplantation from living donors, open heart operations to bone marrow transplantation and other rare operations, some of which were performed for the first time at the level of the Middle East.

Prince Sultan Military Medical City has achieved quantum and quality leaps in procuring the latest medical equipment thanks to Allah and the strong support it has been receiving from the leaders of the nation headed by HRH Prince Salman bin Abdul Aziz, Crown Prince, Deputy Premier and Minister of Defense who has spared no effort in supporting this leading medical facility in order for it to be in the ranks of international military medical institutions in different fields and to realize an advanced level of efficiency and capability to fulfill its leading role at times of peace and war.

My colleagues and I in Prince Sultan Military Medical City have the honor to provide everything that help serve patients in this blessed country. We also hope that this electronic front will make it easy for visitors and users alike to best benefit from the services provided by Prince Sultan Military Medical City.
